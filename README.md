# Mental-Health-Analysis
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 292,364 mental health survey records using MySQL and Excel.

## Project Overview
An interactive Excel dashboard analysing 292,364 global mental health 
survey records. Data was extracted and queried using MySQL then analysed 
and visualised in Microsoft Excel.

## Tools Used
- MySQL — data extraction and querying
- Microsoft Excel — PivotTables, charts and dashboard creation

## Key Questions Analysed
1. Ratio of men to women with mental health history
2. Treatment seeking by gender and occupation
3. Willingness to discuss mental health in interviews
4. Changing habits as a signal of declining mental health
5. Countries with highest mood swing severity
6. Occupation influence on mental health outcomes
7. Family history vs personal mental health history
8. Self-employed vs corporate stress patterns
9. Treatment seeking trends over time
10. Gender differences in mood swing severity

## Key Findings
- 83% of respondents with mental health history are male
- Women seek treatment 23% more than men (69.4% vs 46.3%)
- 80% of all occupations avoid discussing mental health in interviews
- Corporate workers report the highest growing stress at 39.1%
- Treatment seeking increased from 49.7% in 2014 to 100% in 2016


## Data Source
Kaggle Mental Health Survey Dataset
292,364 respondents | 35 countries | 2014-2016
